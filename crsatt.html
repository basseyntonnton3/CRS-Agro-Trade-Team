Who We Are

Agriculture is the backbone of our communities

The CRS Agro Trade Team (CRSATT) is a registered multi-purpose cooperative society serving all 18 local government areas of Cross River State. We are part of the regional South-South farmers' cooperative network, operating under the auspices of Community Hope MPCS Ltd, powered by Financial Inclusion Services (FIS).

"Feeding the Future, Empowering Farmers" — positioning Cross River State farmers at the forefront of Nigeria's agricultural revolution.

Founded with purpose and solidarity by Bassey Nton Nton (Grassroot Advocate), our platform exists to ensure that every CRS farmer has access to the resources, funding, training, and market connections they deserve.
